Delete Your PupInsight Account

You can request account deletion at any time. Deletion follows a 30-day grace period — during this time, sign back into the app to cancel and keep your data.

Option 1: In-App Deletion (Recommended)

  1. Open PupInsight
  2. Go to Profile tab
  3. Tap Account
  4. Tap Delete Account
  5. Type DELETE to confirm

Option 2: Email Request

If you cannot access the app, email pupinsight@outlook.com from the address associated with your account. Include the subject line "Account Deletion Request." We will process your request within 7 business days.

What is permanently deleted after 30 days

  • Your account and profile information
  • All dog profiles, health records, and photos
  • All chat conversations with Hazel and AI history
  • Behavior logs, mood entries, and training data
  • Notification preferences
  • Push notification tokens (deleted immediately, no grace period)

What is anonymized and retained

Some records are retained after deletion, but with your user identifier removed so they are no longer linked to you:

  • Bug reports and feature requests — content only, to improve the product
  • Aggregated usage metrics — for cost analysis and capacity planning
  • Financial transaction records — retained up to 7 years as required by tax and accounting law

Anonymized records are no longer considered personal data under GDPR, CCPA, PIPEDA, and LGPD.

Cancelling a Pending Deletion

You have 30 days to change your mind. Simply sign back into the PupInsight app with your email and password — your deletion will be cancelled automatically and your data preserved.

Active subscriptions: If you have an active subscription, please cancel it through the Google Play Store before deleting your account to avoid further charges. Deleting your PupInsight account does not automatically cancel your Google Play subscription.

Audit & Compliance

We maintain a cryptographically hashed record of deletion requests (no personal data) for up to 3 years to demonstrate compliance with data protection laws such as GDPR, CCPA, PIPEDA, and LGPD.
