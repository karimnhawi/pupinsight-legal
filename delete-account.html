Delete Your PupInsight Account

In-App Deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data directly from the app:

  1. Open PupInsight
  2. Go to Profile tab
  3. Tap Account
  4. Tap Delete Account
  5. Confirm the deletion

What Gets Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently removed within 30 days:

  1. Your account and profile information
  2. All dog profiles, health records, and photos
  3. All chat conversations with Hazel
  4. Behavior logs, mood entries, and training data
  5. Subscription and usage data
  6. Push notification tokens and preferences
Note: If you have an active subscription, please cancel it through the Google Play Store before deleting your account to avoid further charges.

Request Deletion via Email

If you are unable to access the app, you can request account deletion by emailing pupinsight@outlook.com from the email address associated with your account.
